"HAZARD HERO APPLICATION:

The Hazard Hero App – A Mobile Application for Disaster Awareness and Preparedness – was developed by the BS in Information Systems graduates from the College of Computing Studies, JRMSU-Katipunan Campus, to enhance community resilience through technology. This innovative tool provides accessible and user-friendly information on various types of disasters, empowering individuals, families, and communities to take proactive measures and respond effectively during crises. By facilitating disaster awareness and preparedness, the Hazard Hero Application plays a crucial role in equipping citizens to safeguard themselves and their communities in the face of natural hazards.
